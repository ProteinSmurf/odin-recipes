Stew - A healthy dinner for a strong raider!

What is stew?

I think everyone knows what stew is.. if you do not ... you can google ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

PREP TIME: 10 min

COOK TIME: ~5h min

TOTAL TIME: ~5h & 10 min

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 450g meat of choice - beef,lamb,pork,chicken...
  • 225g bacon (optional)
  • 1/2 cabbage
  • 500ml beer
  • 500ml water / meat or vegetable stock

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients together into the pot and mix it well.
  2. Cook at low medium temperature for about 5 hours monitoring it every now and then
  3. Season with salt and pepper when done.
  4. You can also chop up some herbs and add them
